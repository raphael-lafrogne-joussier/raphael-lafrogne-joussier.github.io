I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at CREST-Ecole Polytechnique (IP Paris), under the supervision of Isabelle Mejean. I also am a macroeconomist at the French National Institute for Statistics and Economic Studies (Insee).

I study global value chains, urban and spatial economics.

I received a BSc in mathematics and economics from Ecole Polytechnique in 2019, a MRes in Economics from IP Paris and a Statistician/Economist MSc degree from ENSAE in 2020. From some parallel life, I also hold a BA in Philosophy.
